Source projects:
- Fast-WAM (FastWAM)
  Paper: Fast-WAM: Do World Action Models Need Test-time Future Imagination?
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16666
  License: Apache-2.0
  Note: The files under ``modules/`` are vendored largely verbatim from the
  FastWAM ``src/fastwam/models/wan22`` package to preserve numerical parity.
  This includes the three model variants ``fastwam.py`` (base/uncond),
  ``fastwam_joint.py`` (action attends to all video tokens) and
  ``fastwam_idm.py`` (teacher-forcing video conditioning). The only changes
  applied to the vendored files are:
    * the logger import, redirected from ``fastwam.utils.logging_config`` to
      the local ``._logging`` shim (all files);
    * ``action_dit.py`` only: the relative-path anchor for
      ``action_dit_pretrained_path`` is ``Path(__file__).parents[5]`` instead
      of ``parents[4]`` upstream, because the vendored module sits one
      directory deeper; both resolve to the respective repository root.
  These files are exempt from the repository's string/format pre-commit hooks
  (see ``.pre-commit-config.yaml``) so they stay byte-identical to upstream.
